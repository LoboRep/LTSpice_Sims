PC & PCI cards

Printed circuit boards are connected directly to a motherboard (mainboard) using PC / PCI slot connectors. There exist the PC-AT Standard (also called “ISA”) with a pitch of 2.54mm (0.1 inch) and the PCI Standard with a grid spacing of 1.27mm (0.05 inch) for the gold fingers. Additional there are PCI Express cards (PCIe) with 1.0mm and Mini PCI Express cards (mPCIe) with 0.8mm pitch.

Design-Rules:

  • The entire plug area including the gold fingers must not be covered by solder-stop (see diagram)
  • Plated-through holes within the contact area are not allowed
  • The circuit board surface may be freely selected
  • The gold fingers are manufactured using “hard gold” (electroplated gold), Au thickness 0.8 - 2µm

Bevelling of the gold fingers must be avoided; therefore they must have a spacing of min. 0.7mm from the circuit board contour.
